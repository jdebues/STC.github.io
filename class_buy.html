Broadway Junior Revue: Pure Imagination

This group class is designed for both beginner and intermediate vocal students, with an emphasis on solo performance. Technical skills are taught alongside stage presence to create an end-of-semester recital and to build on personal improvement in Musical Theatre class performances. Confidence is built by students performing weekly in class for their peers who provide positive feedback and support.
